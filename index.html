About

Full disclosure: despite what the landing page of this portfolio may (or may not lol) show, I have never been "cool." This nerdy '80s kid became a nerdy English teacher && now nerds out coding as a nerdy web developer (nerdCount === 4).


Luckily for me, nerdy has come into its own since the '80s, and my web work shows my different facets: nerdy, precise, chaotic, creative, logical, colorful, light, dark, and sometimes even cool, totally different than anyone would expect from this trivia-loving grandma.


I'm in search of an employer who embraces all labels and is wanting to make our beautiful world an even better place. I will work hard and accurately so you can achieve your mission. Hire me and let's do some awesome work together.
